BFPG Talk Resources

The Brisbane Functional Programming Group holds both a monthly talks and hack nights to help people learn functional programing at all levels. We aim to foster an environment friendly to both beginners and industrial users of FP.

You can find details of our upcoming events on Meetup .

This site is a dedicated home to resources for past talks and give an idea of what the plan is for future talks. It also gives a guide to submitting talk ideas that you would like to hear or deliver yourself.
